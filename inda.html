Living Traditions

Arts & Culture

The creative soul of a billion-strong civilisation

💃
Bharatanatyam
One of the oldest classical dance forms, originating in Tamil Nadu's Devadasi tradition — a language of gestures, rhythm and divine expression.
🎨
Madhubani Art
Vibrant folk paintings from Bihar's Mithila region — depicting Hindu mythology, nature and social events in bold geometric patterns.
🎭
Kathakali
Kerala's spectacular classical dance-drama — performers don elaborate costumes and makeup to enact stories from Hindu epics.
🔵
Warli Painting
Ancient tribal art from Maharashtra — simple geometric shapes like circles, triangles and lines create vivid depictions of everyday life.
🥁
Hindustani Music
A 3,000-year-old classical tradition based on ragas — melodic frameworks that evoke specific moods, seasons and times of day.
🧵
Kanchipuram Silk
South India's legendary silk weaving tradition — fine Zari gold work on silk sarees that take weeks to create and last generations.
🌸
Odissi Dance
One of the oldest surviving dance forms, originating in the temples of Odisha — characterised by its distinctive tribhanga posture.
🧘
Yoga
A 5,000-year-old Indian philosophy and practice — combining physical postures, breathing and meditation — now practiced by 300 million worldwide.

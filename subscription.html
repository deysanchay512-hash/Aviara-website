FAQs
Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my AI prediction quota?

On Basic and Standard plans you'll be notified at 80% usage. Additional predictions are available as top-ups or by upgrading your plan.

Is there a free trial?

New accounts receive a 7-day free trial of the Standard plan. No credit card required. You'll be prompted to choose a plan at the end of the trial.

Do annual plans auto-renew?

Yes. Annual plans renew automatically unless cancelled at least 7 days before the renewal date. You'll receive a reminder 14 days before renewal.
